Root Cause Analysis and Corrective Action Systems

What is Root Cause Analysis (RCA)?

Root Cause Analysis (RCA) is a systematic process for identifying the underlying cause of a problem or non-conformance. In the AS9100/IA9100 framework, a robust RCA is the mandatory first step before implementing a Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA) plan.

Our RCA Methodology

  • The 5 Whys: Diving deep into process failures to find the origin point.
  • Fishbone (Ishikawa) Diagrams: Categorizing potential causes to ensure no stone is left unturned.
  • Risk-Based Thinking: Integrating findings back into your QMS to prevent recurrence.

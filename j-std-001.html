J-STD-001 Technical Standards & Mission Assurance

What is the J-STD-001 standard?

J-STD-001 is the industry-leading standard for soldered electrical and electronic assemblies. It provides the materials, methods, and verification criteria for producing high-quality soldered interconnections in mission-critical defense and aerospace hardware.

Why J-STD-001 Compliance Matters

For SMEs in the Defense Industrial Base, compliance isn't just about quality—it's a requirement for Tier-1 prime approval. We help your team bridge the gap in:

  • Process control for soldering materials and methods.
  • Requirements for flux and solder alloy purity.
  • Specialized requirements for high-reliability aerospace applications.

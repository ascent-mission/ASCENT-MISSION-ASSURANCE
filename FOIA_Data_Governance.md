# Regulatory Liability Shield: Data Governance
A major market restraint for public-facing operators (Part 139 and local government contractors) is the conflict between "non-punitive reporting" and "Sunshine/Open Record" laws [18, 19].

**Our Solution:**
We design data workflows that prioritize the "possession and control" of safety data to isolate it from public disclosure statutes [20, 21]. We don't just fix culture; we build legal firewalls that protect your hazard reporters from FOIA and legal discovery.

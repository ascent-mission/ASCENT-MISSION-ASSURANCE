IA9100 Transition: The 2026 Aerospace Standard Evolution

What is IA9100?

IA9100 is the 2026 international revision of the AS9100 aerospace quality standard. It aligns with ISO 9001:2026 and introduces mandatory requirements for cybersecurity, quality culture, and ethical behavior. It is the new benchmark for QMS compliance in the aviation, space, and defense sectors.

Top 5 Changes in IA9100 vs AS9100

Requirement AS9100 (Old) IA9100 (2026)
Cybersecurity Informal/Suggested Mandatory Information Security Clause
Quality Culture Referenced in notes Explicit Leadership Accountability
APQP Integration Optional framework Direct Linkage to Operations (8.1)
Ethics Implicit requirement Formal Ethical Behavior Protocols
Remote Audits Case-by-case Formally Recognized Methodology
